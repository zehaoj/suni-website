Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add cities?

Tap the "+" button in the city list view to add new cities. You can search for cities by name or browse available cities.

How do I enable the widget?

Long press on your home screen, tap the "+" button, search for "Suni", and add the widget. The widget will show weather for your selected cities.

What weather data does the app use?

Suni uses Apple's WeatherKit service to provide accurate, up-to-date weather forecasts. This ensures reliable weather data powered by Apple's weather services.

Is my data stored in the cloud?

No. All your data (cities, preferences, cached weather) is stored locally on your device. We don't sync or store your data on external servers.

How do I restore my in-app purchase?

Go to Settings within the app and tap "Restore Purchases". Your premium features will be restored if you've previously purchased Suni Plus.

üîí Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Suni ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Suni weather application ("the App").

Information We Collect

Location Data

  • City Coordinates: When you add a city to the App, we store the city name and its geographic coordinates (latitude and longitude) that you provide or select.
  • Purpose: We use location coordinates solely to fetch weather data for the cities you choose to monitor.
  • Storage: City data is stored locally on your device using iOS UserDefaults and App Groups (for widget functionality).

Weather Data

  • Cached Weather Information: The App caches weather data locally on your device to improve performance and reduce network usage.
  • Purpose: Cached data allows the App to display weather information quickly and function offline.
  • Storage: Weather data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

App Usage Data

  • City Preferences: We store your list of selected cities, their order, and display preferences locally on your device.
  • Settings: Your app settings (temperature units, distance units, etc.) are stored locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide weather forecasts and current conditions for your selected cities
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Cache data locally to reduce network requests
  • Enable widget functionality to display weather on your home screen

Third-Party Services

Apple WeatherKit

  • The App uses Apple's WeatherKit service to fetch weather data.
  • WeatherKit is provided by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • We do not share your location data with Apple beyond what is necessary to provide weather services.
  • WeatherKit requests are made using coordinates you provide when adding cities.

Firebase Storage (Google)

  • The App may fetch city configuration data and images from Firebase Storage.
  • This service is provided by Google and is subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • We only access publicly available city data and images, not personal information.

OpenWeatherMap (Optional Fallback)

  • The App may use OpenWeatherMap API as an optional fallback weather service.
  • This service is provided by OpenWeatherMap and is subject to their privacy policy.
  • Only city coordinates are sent to this service to retrieve weather data.

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All your personal data (cities, preferences) is stored locally on your iOS device.
  • No Cloud Sync: We do not sync your data to cloud servers or external databases.
  • App Groups: We use iOS App Groups to share data between the main app and widget extension, which remains on your device.
  • Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data stored on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

We only share data as necessary to:

  • Provide weather services through Apple WeatherKit, Firebase Storage, or OpenWeatherMap
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect our rights and safety

In-App Purchases

  • The App offers in-app purchases through Apple's StoreKit.
  • Purchase information is handled by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.
  • We do not have access to your payment information.
  • Purchase status is stored locally on your device to enable premium features.

Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View all data stored by the App (accessible through the App's settings)
  • Delete: Remove cities and clear cached data at any time through the App
  • Control: Manage your privacy settings and data through iOS Settings

Data Retention

  • Local Data: Data stored on your device remains until you delete the App or manually remove cities/settings.
  • Cached Weather: Weather data is cached temporarily and automatically refreshed.
  • No Server Storage: We do not retain your data on external servers.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • For significant changes, we may provide additional notice through the App

International Users

If you are using the App from outside the United States, please note that your data may be processed in the United States or other countries where our service providers operate. By using the App, you consent to the transfer of your information to these countries.

California Privacy Rights

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
